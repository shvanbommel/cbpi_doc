The main features

These are some of CraftBeerPI's great features. Explore the possibilities...

Flexible Kettle Configuration

From simple single kettle preserving cooker to 3 kettle RIMS or HERMS setup

Flexible Brew Step Configuraiton

Configure your own brew steps. From mashing over boiling to whirlpool

Import recipes from Beer.xml and "Kleiner Brauhelfer"

Plan your brew at your favorite tool like BeerSmith or "Kleiner Brauhelfer" and import the recipes to CraftBeerPI

Expandable

Extend the software with new control logics or hardware connectors/p>

Heater, Pump, Valves & Agitator Control

Control your hardware via web interface

PID Controller

The PID Controller takes care to meet the target temperatue of your kettle

Temperature Chart

Temprature data is recorded and displayed as a line chart

Fermentation Control

Define fermentation steps and create the perfect condition for your fermentation
